**Tender Summary: Key Requirements and Conditions**

**I. General Requirements for All Tenders:**

*   **Compliance & Documentation:**  Bidders must submit comprehensive documentation including company registration certificates, tax returns (ITR & GST), work completion certificates, equipment lists, and manpower details.
*   **E-Tendering:**  All submissions must be made online via government e-procurement portals.  Bidders are responsible for managing technical issues.
*   **Earnest Money Deposit (EMD):**  A mandatory EMD is required to demonstrate commitment (specific amounts vary per tender – see below).
*   **Security Deposit/Performance Bank Guarantee:** A financial holdback is required to ensure accountability (specific amounts and types vary per tender – see below).
*   **Two-Part Evaluation:**  Tenders will be evaluated based on technical capabilities and financial proposals.
*   **Accountability & Integrity:** Fair evaluation methods, grievance redressal mechanisms, and (in some cases) annual performance reviews are in place.
*   **Legal & Statutory Compliance:** Bidders must comply with labor laws, safety standards, and environmental regulations.
*   **Financial Penalties:** Penalties will be imposed for delayed completion, substandard work, and non-compliance.
